About TCRB

A review board that doesn't just critique the market—it defines it.

The Cannabis Review Board exists to establish a transparent, data-driven, and culturally aware standard of evaluation for cannabis products, operators, and markets. We are committed to transforming cannabis from a fragmented, perception-driven industry into a measurable, accountable, and editorially documented ecosystem—where quality, safety, and authenticity are not assumed, but verified.

What We Are

TCRB operates at the intersection of consumer protection, industry accountability, cultural documentation, and market intelligence.

Inspired by regulatory bodies that prioritize public safety and standards, and advocacy groups that emphasize equity and access, TCRB advances a new model: institutional-grade cannabis intelligence with editorial independence.

What We Are Not

We are not a blog. Not a dispensary. Not a media company. Not a consultancy that accepts payment in exchange for favorable coverage. TCRB maintains editorial independence from advertisers and operators. Our methodology is documented. Our scoring is consistent. Our conflicts of interest are firewalled.

Core Objectives
01

Standardization of Quality

Develop and enforce a proprietary review framework for cannabis products and retail environments. Score based on composition, experience, transparency, pricing integrity, and cultural alignment.

02

Public-Facing Transparency

Deliver clear, accessible reviews that translate industry complexity into consumer understanding. Build a trusted archive of cannabis intelligence.

03

Industry Accountability

Hold brands, dispensaries, and operators to consistent, visible standards. Reward excellence while exposing mediocrity.

04

Cultural Preservation

Document the evolving identity of cannabis across regions, communities, and generations. Protect legacy influence from being erased by commercialization.

Structural Integrity

TCRB is designed as a dual-entity system to ensure both credibility and operational independence.

For-Profit Arm

TCRB Inc.

Operates as a media, data, and intelligence company. Generates revenue through premium reports, licensing data, brand audits, and partnerships.

Internal review panels modeled after compliance boards and advisory commissions. Built on conflict-of-interest firewalls, review standard consistency, and documented scoring methodology. Editorial independence is structural, not aspirational.

Nonprofit Arm

The Vineyard Foundation

The nonprofit extension of TCRB, dedicated to restoration, education, and equity within the cannabis ecosystem and beyond.

Where TCRB defines the market, The Vineyard Foundation rebuilds the soil it grows from.

Registered as a nonprofit entity (501(c)(3) target). Receives donations, grants, and a percentage of TCRB profits. Clear separation from TCRB's commercial operations prevents pay-to-play influence and biased evaluations.

The Vineyard Foundation — Mission

To reinvest knowledge, resources, and capital into communities impacted by prohibition, systemic neglect, and economic exclusion—while cultivating pathways for ownership, wellness, and environmental harmony.

Equity & Restoration

Support individuals and communities disproportionately affected by cannabis criminalization. Provide pathways into legal industry participation, expungement resources, and workforce development.

Education & Access

Deliver public education on cannabis science, responsible consumption, and market literacy. Bridge the gap between consumer and operator knowledge.

Environmental Stewardship

Promote sustainable cultivation and urban green initiatives. Support projects like Brick & Bloom. Reinforce the connection between cannabis and land.

Wellness & Community

Fund and support initiatives tied to mental health (Still Waters), autism support (Autism Trust Seed Fund), and pet care (Tail End Trust).

The Cannabis Review Board is the lens.
The Vineyard Foundation is the soil.

One defines value. The other restores it.
